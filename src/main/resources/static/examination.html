EXAMINATIONS



Academic Membership: IIE Conduct Two Type of Academic Membership Examination

i. Technician/Diploma Membership Examination (DIPIIE-TMIIE-DMIIE)

Part 1 : Total No. of Paper - 9 (9x100) = 900 Marks
Part 2 : Total No. of Paper = 18(18x100)=1800 Marks
Project Work (1x100)=100 Marks
Total = 2800 Marks
Minimum Pass Marks Required : 40%

ii. Associate Membership Examination (AMIIE)

Section A : Total No. of Paper - 9 (9x100) = 900 Marks
Section B :Total No. of Paper = 18(18x100)=1800 Marks
Project Work:(1x100)=100 Marks
Total = 2800 Marks
Minimum Pass Marks Required :40%
